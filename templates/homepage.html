About This Portal

Built during a summer internship at Cochin Shipyard Limited, this portal centralises the design calculation tools used across the four core engineering disciplines of shipbuilding.

🚢

What This Is

A web-based tool interface for ship design calculations — covering naval architecture, structural, electrical, and mechanical domains. Inputs go in, reports come out.

🏗️

Where It's From

Developed at Cochin Shipyard Limited, India's largest shipyard and the only facility capable of building and repairing the largest vessels that visit Indian ports.

👥

Who Made It

Intern team, Naval Architecture Department, CSL. B.Tech Year 1. Built as a practical tool for design engineers — not a demo, an actual workflow aid.
